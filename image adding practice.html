Dora bujji

Dora the Explorer is an American children's animated television series created by Chris Gifford, Valerie Walsh Valdes and Eric Weiner that premiered on Nickelodeon on August 14, 2000, finished airing on June 5, 2014 on Nickelodeon, and wrapped up on August 9, 2019. The series was produced by Nickelodeon Animation Studio.
